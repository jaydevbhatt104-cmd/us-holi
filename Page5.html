Aaje Holi chhe ane hu tari pase nathi, pan mara dil na badha rang tara naam na chhe. Distance sirf sharir ma chhe, pan mara prem ni gaherai ma tame hamesha najik chho.

Tari smile mara mate sabse sundar rang chhe. Tari vaat, tari hasi, tari narajgi pan mara jeevan ne rangin banave chhe.

Jami lejo sarkhu, smile karta rehjo, ane yaad rakhjo — hu hamesha tari saathe chhu. Aaje virtual gulal chhe, pan agal ni Holi ma hu mara haath thi tara gaal par rang lagavish.

Tame mara jeevan ni sabse pyaari feeling chho. Forever and always. ❤️
